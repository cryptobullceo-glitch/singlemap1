Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Signalmap ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website signalmap.live. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about you when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter or create an account
  • Usage data — pages viewed, time on site, referral source (via anonymous analytics)
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe; we do not store card details
  • Enterprise contact information — name, company, and message when you submit the enterprise contact form

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you the Signalmap weekly intelligence brief (if subscribed)
  • To send email drip sequences related to our product
  • To send prediction resolution alerts (Pro subscribers)
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To improve our service based on usage patterns

3. Email Communications

By subscribing, you agree to receive emails from Signalmap. You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email, or by visiting signalmap.live/unsubscribe.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database hosting (EU/US)
  • Resend — email delivery
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Netlify — website hosting

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address and subscription data for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your data is marked inactive but retained for up to 12 months for compliance purposes. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@signalmap.live.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for site function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Analytics data is anonymous and aggregated.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure database hosting, and access controls. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify subscribers of material changes via email.

10. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@signalmap.live
