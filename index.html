FAQ

Common questions.

Why hiring data? ▾

Job postings are public, legally required disclosures that move well before press releases. A compliance surge at an exchange precedes a regulatory filing. A wave of product hires precedes a launch. We read the signal before the announcement.

How accurate are your predictions? ▾

Every prediction is logged publicly with a deadline and outcome — view the scorecard to see our current hit rate. We don't move deadlines and we don't delete wrong calls.

Which exchanges do you cover? ▾

Currently: Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, OKX, Gemini, Crypto.com, Robinhood, BitMEX, Bitpanda, and Bitvavo. We add exchanges when we can verify a reliable job data source.

How often is data updated? ▾

The scraper runs weekly. The brief goes out every Friday. Pro subscribers see the latest data in the dashboard as soon as each scrape completes.

Can I cancel anytime? ▾

Yes. Pro subscriptions are managed through Stripe. Cancel any time — you keep access until the end of your billing period.
